A Legacy of Culture & Talent

Christ University has a rich history of fostering holistic development through its vibrant co-curricular activities. The university hosts various collegiate and inter-university mega fests annually, which serve as platforms for students to showcase their creativity, critical thinking, leadership, and performance skills.

Key Celebrations

  • Bhasha Utsav: A grand celebration of cultural diversity, language, and ethnicity, where the campus comes alive with traditional attire and ethnic music.
  • Darpan: The mega intra-collegiate talent search event, identifying the best creative talents in dance, music, art, and theater across departments.
  • Inbloom: The annual national-level inter-university cultural festival featuring competitive events that attract colleges from all over India.
  • Blossoms: An inter-deanery competition that brings out outstanding creative talent and healthy competitive spirit among different departments.

Our Vision

By integrating academic excellence with arts, theater, sports, and culture, Christ University Fests aim to cultivate a well-rounded educational experience that celebrates diversity, promotes unity, and nurtures individual excellence.
